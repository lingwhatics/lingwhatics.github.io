1929, originally uploaded by dalmond.

Saturday, I went on my own to map more of the streets above Lawrence between Yonge and Bayview. Heather was off with her friend. I met up with them later in the afternoon to see a Fringe Festival show called Opera on the Rock. It was shown in a pub and the performers were at the bar and walking around the room. It was funny and they sang almost all of the show.

The evening was spent sitting in the apartment just hanging out. Today, we went down to the Harbourfront Centre to see what was on but having arrived too late/early we left and took a walk up Bathurst. Ending our trek at the subway and getting some groceries. It was a nice weekend, though some rain would be welcome.
