So, it has been a bit longer than I was going to take. I have done many things since my last post. I actually went to see An Inconvenient Truth. And it was a scary movie. I suppose there were lots of graphs which could be taken out of proportion but why would you lie about climate change.

Last Friday, Rhoda had a house warming party. That was a fun time. We all played Cranium which I think is getting a bit old as a game. Next time, I hope that we will have the Canadian Trivia Game. Heather and I had a nice walk to Kensington from there and I managed to catch the last bus home.

I feel like I got up the next morning and watched the World Cup game that was on and then mowed the lawn. I also worked. After work, I went to Heather's and we watched most of a Bridge Too Far. And finished it in the morning over waffles. We followed that up with a walk through downtown. (As always the photos are on flickr.)

I got a new project Monday for engineering work and started painting my aunt's kitchen at night. Tuesday, we had a good dinner and walk over at my dad's.

Tonight, Tyson and I played some video games. It was great to not have to do anything strenuous. I get the joy of working the next three nights, so I don't think there will be many video games.
