TFC v. Impact game, originally uploaded by dalmond.

Tuesday night was the final game in the Canadian Championship (Soccer). It was between Toronto FC and Montreal Impact. The game ended in a tie and therefore Montreal were the champions. Unfortunately, Toronto didn't really play past the first half hour. They spent a lot of time passing the ball in Montreal's end, but none of the players seemed to take responsibility for shooting on net.
