Diesel Theatre - Evil Dead
Originally uploaded by dalmond.

Last night, we saw Evil Dead The Musical. The show was really funny. I went with Heather and her brother. The play house (Diesel) was really nice the set up was neat. There were "tables" with four people each. I call them "tables" because they were small and only one person could put their legs under. However, we were in the back row and the view was really good.

They served drinks and food. Both were very good. All in all it was a really good theatre experience and I would go see another show there for sure.
