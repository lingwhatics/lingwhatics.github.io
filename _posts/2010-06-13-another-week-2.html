Saturday, we got up early to watch World Cup. I had to take a computer for my sister up to my grandma's and we did some grocery shopping. Of course, we watched England-US in the afternoon. After a delicious pizza, we went down to the Harbourfront Centre for a concert by Les Trois Accords, part of Franco-Fête.
