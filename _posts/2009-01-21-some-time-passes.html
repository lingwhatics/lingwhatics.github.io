I probably shouldn't already be counting down to the end of the semester but the first day seemed like a good time to start and here I am at the end of the 8th of 64 class days and then 3 exams. Last week, I wasn't really sure as to what my classes would be like and they all seemed a bit boring. However, 3/4 have picked up this week and show some promise and the 4th prof will return next week from the middle of nowhere, so maybe that one will be good too.

This semester, I'm working Monday to Friday and it seems like a good plan. I get lots of hours done earlier in the days and so am free to do work and such in the late afternoon/evening. I also end up with weekends free.

Soon I need to get out and take photos. I haven't taken that many since I've been in Calgary.
