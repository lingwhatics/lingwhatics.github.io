The Chairs Are Where the People Go: How to Live, Work, and Play in the City by Misha Glouberman
My rating: 4 of 5 stars

Enjoyable meandering through thoughts and stories. My favorite parts are “Asking a Good Question”, ”Conferences Should Be an Exhilarating Experience” and “Failure and Games”.

Asking a Good Question should be presented as part of an introduction to most or all public lectures that will have a Q&A portion afterward.
