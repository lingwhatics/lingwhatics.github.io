I hear the motorcycles coming
They're coming down the hill
They're waiting for the light to change
I hope it never will

I hear the motorcycles coming
Their mufflers blaring full
The groups are getting larger
In summer they're larger still

I hear the motorcycles coming
They never seem to stop
All day and night to their delight
Up and down the hill they chop.
