Special Parking For..., originally uploaded by dalmond.

Special Parking for... Nothing. You know why? Because nothing large happens outside of summertime on the waterfront. That won't stop Waterfron(t)Toronto from pretending that it is a hip and happening place all year round. (I wish there was another 't' in the name)

Of all the people I saw down there, who weren't skating, one was an obvious tourist, a few were joggers and the others were walking their dogs. I am willing to bet the dog walkers live down there because there isn't lots of space down there.
