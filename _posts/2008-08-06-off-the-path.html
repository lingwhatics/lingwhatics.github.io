Off the path, originally uploaded by dalmond.

Sunday, we went for a walk (though i guess people would call it a hike) in Forks of the Credit Provincial Park and it was mostly nice. The only downside were the mosquitoes that started attacking about halfway through the 8.2km walk.

We started on a trail and saw the waterfalls. After the falls, we continued along a trail and somehow ended up back at the parking lot.

Other than mosquitoes, we only saw a garter snake, a toad and many crickets.
