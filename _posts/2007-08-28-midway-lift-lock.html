Midway lift lock
Originally uploaded by dalmond.

On Saturday we went to Peterborough to see this... the lift lock. It is the tallest hydraulic lift lock in the world. (19.8 metres if i remember the sign correctly.) It was a nice drive. Nice to get out to the city and walkabout though we only walked a little. We drove the 4th line road from Millbrook for a long time and ended up on Steeles Avenue. Who knew that the 4th line turned into Steeles. Or that Steeles is the 4th line.

Anyway, it was a nice day trip.
